Data Scientist at Wanderu Marcus Way's workshop on webscraping. Wanderu uses web scraping to store bus ticket data and automate the purchasing of tickets through partnering sites. This workshops takes us through the process of aquiring data from the web and building a model to predict where article titles originate from using Naive Bayes Classification.

Marcus is a TA for Harvard's Data Science class, and is featured in a youtube video with over 17 million views:
